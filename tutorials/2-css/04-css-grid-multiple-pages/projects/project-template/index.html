Project Title

Denielle Sambilay is a Filipina visual development and concept artist based in the Bay Area and is currently pursuing a Digital Media Art degree at San Jose State University. She has a strong passion for storytelling, and her work plays with capturing stories through color, texture, and light with unique visual styles. She draws inspiration from her childhood and the fictional tales she grew up watching and listening to with her family, sparking her admiration for film and animation. Denielle likes working with teams and collaborating on design projects, animated films, and games. She is currently working on a student-led game project as a concept artist and designer at SJSU. In her spare time, Denielle likes to open blind boxes, karaoke, play the drums, and make drinks for her friends and family.
